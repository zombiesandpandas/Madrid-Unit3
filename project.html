Why everyone loves bioshock

Bioshock may seem like your ordinary game, where you kill enemies with weapons and your goal is to resque whomever. The uniqueness about BioShock is that the game is based about a underwater city that went totally downhill because of abuse of the plasmids and ADAM. The people became splicers, total crazy splicers.You are trying to stop all of this, you are trying to get out, and you are on an adventure, where you yourself will have to use plasmids and ADAM to fight off the splicers. As you go along the way you eventually meet these little girks who have deep glowing eyes. These girls are the little sisters, they were kidnapped when rapture was at its best, they trained the little ones to be collectors of adam. You get the option to kill or save them, but if you get to close to these little sisters, their "big daddy" will attack. This game makes you question every single thing you do, this game makes you do things that will change your outcome. Based on the desicions you make, the game can end in 2 different ways.
